# Track Market

Track financial markets by researching stock prices, charts, news, key opinion leader (KOL) commentary, and providing actionable alerts.

## Capabilities

### Stock Price & Charts
- Look up current and historical stock prices for any ticker symbol
- Summarize recent price action, trends, and key technical levels (support/resistance, moving averages)
- Compare performance across multiple tickers or indices

### News & Headlines
- Search for the latest market-moving news for a given stock, sector, or the broader market
- Summarize earnings reports, SEC filings, analyst upgrades/downgrades, and macro events
- Highlight sentiment shifts and unusual activity

### KOL Articles & Commentary
- Find and summarize recent posts, articles, or commentary from notable investors, analysts, and financial influencers
- Sources include financial blogs, Substack, X/Twitter threads, and analyst notes
- Surface contrarian or consensus views on specific stocks or themes

### Alerts & Watchlist
- Flag significant price moves, volume spikes, or breaking news for watched tickers
- Highlight upcoming catalysts (earnings dates, Fed meetings, ex-dividend dates)
- Provide a concise risk/opportunity summary when notable events occur

## Usage Examples

- `/track-market AAPL` — Get a quick overview of Apple's price, recent news, and KOL sentiment
- `/track-market TSLA news` — Focus on the latest Tesla headlines and analyst commentary
- `/track-market portfolio AAPL MSFT GOOG` — Compare price action across multiple holdings
- `/track-market alerts NVDA` — Check for any notable moves or upcoming catalysts for NVIDIA

## Notes

- Uses web search and web fetch to gather real-time data; results depend on publicly available sources.
- This skill does not execute trades or connect to brokerage accounts.
